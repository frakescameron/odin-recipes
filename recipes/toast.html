Sometimes, the simplest things are the most satisfying. Our classic buttered toast is warm, golden, and perfectly crisped on the outside while remaining soft and fluffy inside. Made with thick-sliced bread and real butter, this comfort food staple delivers a rich, creamy flavor in every bite.

Whether it's part of a hearty breakfast or a cozy midnight snack, buttered toast offers the nostalgic taste of home. It's the perfect base for jams, honey, or simply enjoyed on its own with a hot cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • slice of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
    •

    Steps

    1. Put the bread in the toaster, and wait 3 minutes
    2. Take the toast out of the toaster and apply the butter.
    3. Plate and serve!
